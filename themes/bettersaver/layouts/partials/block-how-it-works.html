Tell us a little about you

The right KiwiSaver fund depends on your life — not just your age. We start by understanding your goals, comfort with risk, and what matters to you ethically.

What we ask
  • Are you saving for your first home or for retirement?
  • How do you feel about investment ups and downs?
  • Do you want to avoid things like weapons, gambling, or fossil fuels?

It only takes a couple of minutes — and it's the foundation for better advice.

We find you a better fund - and handle the switch

Most people are in a fund that's just not right for them. We analyse the entire market to find one that suits you better.

How we do it
  • We analyse over 300 KiwiSaver funds
  • We look at performance, risk, fees, and where your money is invested
  • We use independent data from Morningstar
  • Every fund is rated from A+ to F, and we only recommend the best fit
If you're ready to switch
  • You give us the details we need (IRD number, ID, etc.)
  • We handle the switch — fast, secure, and hassle-free
  • You'll get updates along the way so you're never left wondering

It's simple, stress-free, and could make a real difference to your future.

Keep your KiwiSaver working harder

Switching is just the start. We help you stay on track - and reward you for it. 

What you get
  • A dashboard that shows how your KiwiSaver is tracking
  • Tools to help you stay on course toward your goal - first home or retirement
  • Helpful nudges to keep you moving forward

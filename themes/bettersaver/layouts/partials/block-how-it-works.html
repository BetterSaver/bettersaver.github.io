Tell us a little about you

The right KiwiSaver fund depends on your life — not just your age. We start by understanding your goals, comfort with risk, and what matters to you ethically. 

What we ask
  • Are you saving for your first home or for retirement?
  • How do you feel about investment ups and downs?
  • Do you want to avoid things like weapons, gambling, or fossil fuels?

It only takes a couple of minutes — and it's the foundation for better advice.

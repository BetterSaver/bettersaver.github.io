BetterSaver fund ratings explained

We grade every fund A+ to F across four core checks — Performance, Fees, Consistency, and Quality — comparing each fund only to its peers in the same risk category (Defensive, Conservative, Balanced, Growth, Aggressive).

To be recommended, a fund needs at least 5 years of history and an overall grade of B or above.

A+ Top pick. Stands out against peers overall.
A Top performer. Performs well overall.
B Quality option. Meets our bar to be recommended.
NR Not recommended. Doesn't meet our criteria (grades C through F shown as NR).

Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future returns. Ratings are updated at least annually.
